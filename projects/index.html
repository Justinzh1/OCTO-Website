Projects

This is how we improve students' lives.

Berkeley Mobile


The Berkeley Mobile Application has a plethora features, including real-time BearTransit Routes, Dining hall menus (including breakfast, lunch, dinner, and late night menus for all dining halls), schedules for all of our public athletic facilities, and hours for every library on campus. We believe that the application is an absolute necessity for the busy Berkeley Student. Download the app for free for iOS here and for Android here.

BerkeleyTime


BerkeleyTime is a website that allows you to search the 8000+ courses from the Berkeley catalog and figure out what you're interested in. You can look up grade distributions and enrollment history for these classes and even explore the campus map to figure out where everything is. We believe that BerkeleyTime will change the way you do things at Berkeley. Visit the site by going here.

Project Hermione


Every bear has been a situation where an emergency prevents him or her from going to class.Using state-of-the-art Computer Vision technology, our software developers were able to automate the lecture recording process so that it is easier for the administration to pay for Lecture Casting and easier for professors to set up the process.
